M.E.F. Logging PostSharp Tool

In 2011 I had an interview with Microsoft's "Flight" team.  During this interview, I was questioned regarding their M.E.F. framework, which at the time, I knew nothing about.  After the interview, I decided to create a new logging tool, which uses this very cool M.E.F. framework, coupled with PostSharp.  The end-result is a simple but powerful attribute, which allows you to simply drop the attribute onto any of your projects at the namespace level, immediately injecting your project with logging capabilities. 

I have updated my M.E.F. logging attribute to now support Microsoft's Enterprise logging framework too! :)
